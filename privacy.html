Privacy Policy

How infoblade handles your data.

Last updated: June 3, 2026

The short version: We offer optional user accounts (email and password only). We never sell or share your personal data with third parties. You can delete your account at any time.

Who we are

infoblade is an AI-powered financial news analysis platform. We aggregate publicly available news headlines and use artificial intelligence to identify potential market impacts. All content is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice.

Information we collect

1. Account data (optional)

Creating an account is entirely optional. If you do create one, we store your email address and a securely hashed password. We never store plaintext passwords. Your email is used solely for authentication and password recovery — never for marketing.

2. Watchlist (account holders)

If you have an account, your watchlist is synced to our secure database so it persists across devices. Without an account, watchlist data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to our servers.

3. IP addresses (automatic, temporary)

Your IP address is temporarily stored for rate limiting purposes, to prevent abuse and ensure fair access for all users. This data is automatically purged on a rolling basis and is never used to identify you personally or shared with third parties.

4. Usage analytics (anonymous)

We use Google Analytics to understand how the app is used in aggregate: which pages are visited, how often features are used, and general usage patterns. Google Analytics collects anonymized data including approximate location (country/city level), device type, and page interaction events. This data is never linked to any personal identity.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. AI analysis requests

When you use AI Chat, the content of your query and relevant context are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate a response. No personal information is included in these requests. Anthropic's privacy policy governs their handling of this data: anthropic.com/privacy.

Information we do NOT collect

  • Your name, physical address, or phone number
  • Financial account information or investment history
  • Precise location data
  • Payment information
  • Any data from your contacts, camera, or microphone
  • Any data sold or shared with advertisers or data brokers

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic — AI analysis engine. Privacy policy
  • Supabase — Authentication and database infrastructure. Stores user accounts, watchlists, and application data. Privacy policy
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous usage analytics. Privacy policy
  • Vercel — Hosting and serverless infrastructure. Privacy policy
  • Yahoo Finance — Live stock price data. Privacy policy
  • Polymarket — Prediction market odds data. Privacy policy

Data retention

Rate limiting records (IP-based) are automatically purged on a rolling basis. AI-generated market predictions are stored indefinitely for accuracy tracking purposes — these records contain no personal information.

Account data (email address and hashed password) and watchlist data are retained until you request deletion of your account.

Your rights and account deletion

If you have created an account, you have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data (email, hashed password, watchlist). To do so, email us at sharedbuisness2024@gmail.com and we will process your request within 30 days.

If you have not created an account, we hold no personal data tied to your identity. Any questions about IP-related data can also be directed to that address.

Children's privacy

infoblade is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has created an account or provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
