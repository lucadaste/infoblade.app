How it works
01

We read the news

Every day, infoblade ingests hundreds of global news sources: geopolitics, macro data, supply chains, earnings, natural events, and on-chain activity, then filters for what actually moves markets.

02

AI reasons through it

We identify which events carry real economic weight, then traces the full chain reaction to sectors, specific stocks, digital assets, and prediction market odds.

03

You get clarity

No jargon, no noise. Across stocks, crypto, and prediction markets: each event explained in plain English, with sectors, tickers, and confidence levels that we log and track over time.

What you get, across all tools

Full chain reasoning

We never stop at the headline. Every event is traced from cause to economic effect: which sectors move, which tickers are exposed, and why. Across stocks, crypto, and prediction markets.

News vs. market odds

On the Prediction Markets page, we cross-reference Polymarket crowd odds with live news signals, surfacing when the market is pricing something the news doesn't yet support, or vice versa.

Source-weighted accuracy

Not all sources are equal. Every analysis logs which outlets backed each call. The Accuracy History page tracks which sources consistently lead to correct outcomes, and weights future analysis accordingly.

No recommendations, ever

We give you the context, the reasoning, and the track record across every tool on the platform. The decision is always yours. Informed, not directed.
